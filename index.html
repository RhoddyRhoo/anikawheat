As a current student at Edith Cowan University, I have developed a passion for creating user-focused digital experiences that blend form and function. While I am still early in my professional career, I have already built this very portfolio website and I am eager to apply and expand my skills in a collaborative, fast- paced environment. I have hands-on experience in 3D modelling and foundational skills in coding (HTML/CSS).

As someone with an interest in Games and interactive design, my vision is to create digital experiences that engage, empower and inspire users. Whether it's a portfolio website or an interactive interface, I aim to design with intention, prioritising usability, accessability and immersiveness. Web development has helped me sharpen these skills and given me a strong foundation in how people interact with technology. In the long term, I hope to bring these principles into the game design space, developing games and interactive media that are both technically solid and emotionally impactful. I am fascinated by the overlap between responsive web design and user-centred game mechanics; both require an understanding of audience, pacing, feedback and flow. By continuing to grow as a developer now, I am building the tools I need to eventually design game experiences that are not only fun and beautiful, but also thoughtful and accessible to all.
